Hard mode uses a range of 1 to 100 but only gives you 5 attempts. Binary search normally requires 7 guesses for a range of 100 (since 2^7 = 128 > 100), so 5 attempts is genuinely difficult. You must execute binary search perfectly every single time — one suboptimal guess likely costs you the game. Start at 50 without exception. Round midpoints down (e.g., midpoint of 51–100 is 75). There is zero room for gut-feeling guesses.
