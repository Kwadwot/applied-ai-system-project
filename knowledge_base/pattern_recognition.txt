Track your narrowing range explicitly after every guess. If you guessed 30 and got "Too High," your upper bound is now 29. If you then guessed 10 and got "Too Low," your lower bound is now 11. The secret must be between 11 and 29, so your next guess should be 20 (the midpoint). Never guess a number outside your known active range — you already have evidence ruling those numbers out. Writing (low, high) after each guess keeps you organized and prevents wasted guesses.
