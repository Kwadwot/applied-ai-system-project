Easy mode uses a range of 1 to 20 and gives you 6 attempts. Binary search can find any number in this range in at most 5 guesses (since 2^5 = 32, which is greater than 20). This means you have a comfortable buffer. Start at 10. If "Too High," try 5; if "Too Low," try 15. You have room for one imperfect guess, but stay disciplined with the midpoint strategy rather than guessing randomly.
