When you are running out of attempts, calm and precise midpoint guessing is more critical than ever. Do not make emotional or instinct-based guesses. Calculate your exact current range: the lower bound is one above your last "Too Low" guess, and the upper bound is one below your last "Too High" guess. Guess the exact midpoint of this range. Avoid guessing numbers at the extremes of your range unless the midpoint naturally falls there.
