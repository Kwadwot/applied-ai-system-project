Your score is directly tied to how quickly you win. Winning on attempt 1 earns the most points; each additional attempt reduces the points awarded for winning. Too Low guesses always cost 5 points. Too High guesses on even-numbered attempts earn 5 bonus points, while Too High guesses on odd-numbered attempts lose 5 points. The clearest path to a high score is winning as fast as possible by using binary search — speed matters more than managing the high/low bonus.
