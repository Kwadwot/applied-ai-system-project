Normal mode uses a range of 1 to 50 and gives you 8 attempts. Binary search guarantees a solution in at most 6 guesses (since 2^6 = 64, which is greater than 50). With 8 attempts available and only needing 6, you have a two-guess buffer for mistakes. Start at 25. After each feedback, track your bounds: your lower bound rises after every "Too Low," your upper bound falls after every "Too High." Keep guessing the midpoint of your current range.
